First Encounter Of The Tasty Enemies

Do you see this little guy? Its the first of many Bugsnax you will see on your adventure around Snaktooth island. Bugsnax are sentient food that are shapped like bugs. Your goal throughout the game is to capture these Bugsnax, but it will not be that easy. Most Bugsnax dont want to be captured so you will have to find a way to capture them. Many of the ingame mechanics for capturing bugsnax will be food related.

Delicious Enemies Many Ways To Catch

This the the first trap you get to capture Bugsnax. You can place it down and it opens like a net. If a Bugsnac gets close you can press a button to close the trap and capture it just like that. Some Bugsnax like the Bunger for example. Its a big burger with fries for legs. When it sees the trap it will just knock it over. The easiest way to capture it is to load your slingshot with kecthup and shoot it at the ground near the trap. The Bunger will ignore the trap and go to the kecthup so you can close the trap on the Bunger.

What To Do With Your Freshly Caught Bugsnax

Now that you have a few Bugsnax what do you do? well on Snactooth island there is a town called Snacburg that is in shambles. The Grumbuses that live there are all starving because they lack the capturing skills needed to catch their own Bugsnax. In town you can get odd jobs from the people in town from feeding them a certain Bugsnax or just showing them a rare one. So mostly you will take your newly captured Bugsnax into town to feed them to the residents
